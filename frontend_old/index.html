Full Visibility into Your Health Data

SecureMed AI is built on a principle of radical transparency. Every piece of health data you submit passes through 4 distinct layers, each with explicit controls. Your raw data never leaves your device unencrypted, and your conversations stay on your local machine via Ollama.

Your Data Journey — 4 Layers
1
Layer 1
Data Trust & Ingestion
Your vital is validated, checked for duplicates, and processed through our **Anti-Hacking Noise Protocol**. We apply calibrated medical-grade noise (Differential Privacy) to ensure models never see your raw data. Then it's encrypted with AES-256-GCM.
Noise Injection Consent Check AES-256 Encrypt Audit Logged
2
Layer 2
Prediction & Causality
Encrypted data is loaded into an in-memory Health Graph. Rule-based clinical checks run immediately. T-GNN and Causal AI models run as background tasks. No data is sent externally.
Local Only Trust-Weighted In-Memory
3
Layer 3
Explainability
Findings are translated into structured reports with SHAP feature scores, confidence levels, and causal pathways. Differential privacy noise is added to any aggregated statistics before display.
Differential Privacy Local Only Report Logged
4
Layer 4
Conversational AI
Reports are passed as context to your local Ollama LLM (llama3). Your chat messages and health summaries are processed entirely on your own machine — nothing is sent to any cloud AI service.
100% Local LLM No Cloud AI Chat Logged
Where Does Your Data Go?
Your Device (SQLite)
Always Local
All vital records, trust scores, the Health Graph, and explainability reports are stored encrypted on your own machine. Nothing is replicated elsewhere by default.
📁 ./securemed.db · AES-256-GCM
Ollama (Local LLM)
Your Machine
Chat messages and health summaries are sent to Ollama running locally at localhost:11434. The AI model (llama3) runs on your hardware — no internet connection required for AI.
🦙 localhost:11434 · llama3:latest
Audit Log
Local File
Every system action — ingestion, consent changes, alerts, chat requests — is appended to an immutable local log. You can view the full audit trail anytime. No deletes permitted.
📋 ./data/audit/audit.jsonl · append-only
Wearable APIs
Opt-in Only
Fitbit and Apple Health integrations are disabled until you explicitly grant consent in the Consent Manager. Each data stream is controlled independently.
🔒 Requires consent grant per stream
Google Gemini API
Config Opt-in
Only used if you set LLM_PROVIDER=gemini in your .env file. Currently disabled — you are using local Ollama. If enabled, only anonymised health summaries (not raw vitals) would be sent.
☁️ Currently: INACTIVE (using Ollama)
Third Parties / Ads
Never
Your health data is never sold, shared with advertisers, analytics platforms, insurance companies, or any third party. There are no telemetry calls, no tracking pixels, no analytics SDKs.
🚫 Zero external data sharing
Privacy Guarantees
🔐
AES-256-GCM Encryption
Every record is encrypted at rest with your unique master key. The key never leaves your machine.
🛡️
Anti-Hacking Protocol
We add noise to individual records at ingestion. Models and GNNs only process this 'privatized' view, preventing data theft via model weights.
🎲
Differential Privacy
Calibrated Laplace noise is added to any aggregated stats before they are displayed, preventing re-identification.
🚨
Red Flag Guarantee
Emergency alerts (e.g. SpO₂ < 90%) are never suppressed — not even by a low trust score. Safety always wins.
📋
Immutable Audit Trail
Every action is appended to a local log. No entry can be deleted or modified — providing a full chain of custody.
